Our Journey

From a Small Kitchen to Your Favorite Delivery

Bitez started in 2018 with a simple idea: bring restaurant-quality pizza and fast food to every doorstep with freshness and flavor that excites. What began as a single kitchen has grown into a beloved brand serving thousands of happy customers every day.

We source only the finest ingredients — from hand-tossed dough to farm-fresh vegetables and premium meats. Every recipe is crafted with care, and every order is prepared with the same passion that started it all.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped Bitez into what it is today.

2018

First Kitchen Opens

Bitez was born in a small kitchen with a big dream — to serve the best pizza and fast food in town. We started with just 5 menu items and a handful of loyal customers.

2020

Online Delivery Launch

We launched our own delivery platform, making it easier than ever to enjoy Bitez from home. Our customer base grew rapidly as we expanded our menu and delivery radius.

2022

50K Happy Customers

Reached the milestone of 50,000 satisfied customers. We introduced new categories like burgers, pasta, and desserts — becoming a go-to for fast food lovers everywhere.

2024

3 New Locations

Expanded to 3 new cities with state-of-the-art kitchens. Our commitment to quality and innovation continues to drive us forward every single day.

2026

The Future Is Bright

Today, Bitez is a trusted name with over 120 menu items, a growing family of food lovers, and a vision to keep pushing the boundaries of great taste.
