Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common things our customers ask.

🍕 What are your delivery hours?
We deliver every day from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM. For late-night cravings, select locations stay open until 12:00 AM. You can place your order online or through our mobile app.
🚚 How long does delivery take?
Most orders arrive within 30–40 minutes. During peak hours (weekends 6–9 PM), delivery may take up to 50 minutes. We always keep you updated with real-time tracking via SMS or the app.
💰 Do you offer any discounts or loyalty programs?
Yes! Sign up for our Bitez Rewards program to earn points on every order. You also get a 10% discount on your first order when you subscribe to our newsletter. Check our Offers page for weekly deals.
🌱 Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?
Absolutely! We offer a wide range of vegetarian pizzas, plant-based burgers, and vegan-friendly sides. All our vegetarian and vegan options are clearly marked on the menu with a special icon.
📦 Can I order for a large group or event?
Yes! We offer bulk catering and party platters for events of all sizes. Contact our catering team directly at least 24 hours in advance for custom orders, discounts, and delivery coordination.
